Question 30

Explain the advantages of layered approach, with a diagram.

30.1 Layered Approach:

Refer 22nd Question & Answer or Click Here

30.2 Advantages of Layered Approaches are as follows:

i. It Provides modularity and clear interfaces.

ii. Implementation simplicity, maintainability, flexibility, and scalability are maintained.

iii. Layered approach supports portability.

iv. It Provides robustness and preserves stability.

v. It is a Time tested approach.

30.3 Draw Backs/ Disadvantages of Layered Approaches are as follows:

i. Data overhead and processing, due to the duplication of functionality.

ii. The more layers we have, the more risks we have for things to breakdown or data to get lost.

iii. Various issues regarding Higher Layer Vs Lower Layers

iv. Results in complex exploitation of user intensive application.

v. This leads to Sluggish operation modes on various applications.
