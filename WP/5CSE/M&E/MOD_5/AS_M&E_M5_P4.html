Question 12

What is TECSOK? Explain the services offered by TECSOK. (10-Marks) (10a)

(Dec.2018/Jan.2019)

TECHNICAL CONSULTANCY SERVICE ORGANIZATION OF KARNATAKA (TECSOK)

These organizations are highly useful to entrepreneurs in providing many services and are found in almost all the states. It was established in 1976 by Gov. of Karnataka. It’s office is located in Nrupatunga road, Bangalore. It Helps entrepreneur in preparation of feasible reports at a subsidized cost.

Nature of support

Multi-disciplinary Technical, Industrial and Management Consultancy.

Objectives

i. To provide reliable consultancy support for entrepreneurs to start up self-employment ventures in Karnataka.

ii. To provide Consultancy services to various Departments and agencies of state and central Gov.

Functions

i. Identifications of project ideas and selections of investment opportunities

ii. Selection of suitable locations for setting up industrial units.

iii. Conducting market surveys, industrial potential surveys.

iv. Preparation of detailed techno –economic feasibility report/detailed project report

v. Energy audit and conservation.

vi. Modernization studies.

vii. Dissemination of info on industrial policies and procedures central as well as state government.

viii. Assistance into government in providing info about new policies, programs and schemes.

ix. Assist in obtaining necessary licenses and clearance.


Question 13

Explain the aims and objectives of KIADB. (6-Marks) (10b)

(Dec.2018/Jan.2019)

KARNATAKA INDUSTRIAL AREAS DEVELOPMENT BOARD (KIADB):

It is wholly owned infrastructure agency of Gov. of Karnataka, set up under Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Act of 1996. This Board function as per statutory provisions, rules, and regulations enacted under them, Board of members meet regularly to take decisions and monitor functions.

KIADB holds pride in being the first Gov. organization in Karnataka to obtain ISO 900] certification in the year 1997, Now KIADB is following ISO 9001:2000 module covering its functions of land Acquisition, development and allotment functions in Bangalore Urban and Rural areas.

13.1 Objectives

i. Promote rapidly and orderly development of industries in the state.

ii. Assist in implementation of policies of Gov. within the purview of KIAD act.

iii. Facilitate for establishing infrastructure projects.

iv. Function on corporate lines “no profit, no loss”.

13.2 Functions

i. Acquire land and form industries areas.

ii. Provide all infrastructure to such industrial areas.

iii. Acquire land for single unit complexes.

iv. Acquire land for Gov. agencies for their schemes and infrastructure projects

13.3 Some projects of KIADB executed

i. Acquisition of about 4316.25 acres of lands for Bangalore International Airport Itd.

ii. Acquisition of about 1850 acres of lands for Harborat Tadri in Uttar Kannada district.

iii. Acquisition of about 1958 acres of land for M/s MRPL at Mangalore and rehabilitation of 610 displaced families with modern infrastructure.

iv. Acquiring and developing of about 430 acres of land for M/s Toyota in Bidadiat Bangalore Dist.
